Internship Categories

At AI Intern, our aim is to make internships easy and accessible, especially for first-generation students. Here's some information to help you find the perfect internship.

Popular Internship Categories

We have discovered the most sought-after internship categories among students:

  • Data Science Internship
  • Web Developer Internship
  • Java Developer Internship
  • Marketer Internship
  • AI Research Internship

Challenges in Finding Internships

Many students face these challenges when searching for internships:

  • Not having the required skills beforehand
  • Not knowing which internships align with their existing skills

The Ideal Internship

An ideal internship is one that meets the student’s individual needs and aligns perfectly with their existing skills. We focus on ensuring this alignment for first-generation students.

Benefits of PM Internship Scheme:

  • 12-month real-life experience in India's top companies
  • Monthly assistance of ₹4500 by Government + ₹500 by industry
  • One-time grant of ₹6000 for incidentals
  • Insurance coverage for every intern under PM Jeevan Jyoti Bima Yojana & PM Suraksha Bima Yojana

Internship Requirement

Before applying for internships, students should:

  • Have a basic education level
  • Choose their education level (12th grade, college, or graduated)
  • Focus on skills that are required for their internships

We will then recommend internships based on these criteria to make the process easier. Our aim is to make internships easy and accessible for first-generation students who don't have enough facilities or enough information available.

If a person is lacking the skills needed, we would recommend skills and the platform they can learn them on.
